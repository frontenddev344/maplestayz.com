Canada’s Premier Spa Hotels

Explore Maple Stayz’s guide to the best spa hotels in Canada! When you’re seeking relaxation and rejuvenation across the Great White North, our curated selection of spa hotels offers the perfect retreat. From the serene settings of British Columbia to the tranquil spots in Quebec, these hotels provide luxurious spa treatments, soothing amenities, and a touch of Canadian hospitality. Discover the ultimate relaxation experience and find your ideal spa getaway with our top picks.

The Top Spa Resorts In Canada

Fairmont Banff Springs, Banff

Price per Night: CAD $400+

Known as the "Castle in the Rockies," Fairmont Banff Springs offers a luxurious spa experience with stunning mountain views.

  • Willow Stream Spa with a range of treatments
  • Stunning mountain views and serene environment
  • Multiple dining options and recreational facilities

Fairmont Banff Springs, known as the "Castle in the Rockies," provides a luxurious retreat with its Willow Stream Spa. The spa offers a range of treatments, including massages and facials, in a serene setting with stunning mountain views. The hotel features elegant rooms, and guests can dine at several venues, including the Château Dining Room and the casual Brassierie. The property also includes a fitness center, an indoor pool, and a nearby golf course, making it a perfect destination for relaxation and recreation.
