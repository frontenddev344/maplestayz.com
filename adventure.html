Adventure Hotels

Canada’s Premier Adventure Hotels

Explore Maple Stayz’s guide to top adventure hotels in Canada! For those seeking thrilling experiences across the Great White North, our curated selection of adventure hotels offers the perfect base. From the rugged Rockies to the stunning coastlines, these hotels provide easy access to exciting outdoor activities and breathtaking landscapes, all while delivering Canadian hospitality and comfort. Explore our top picks to find the ideal spot for your next adventure-filled getaway.

The Top Adventure Hotels In Canada

The Fairmont Chateau Whistler

Price per Night: CAD $350+

The Fairmont Chateau Whistler offers direct access to Whistler Blackcomb and stunning mountain views.

  • Ski-in/ski-out access to Whistler Blackcomb
  • Luxurious rooms and suites with mountain views
  • On-site adventure concierge for activity planning

The Fairmont Chateau Whistler is a premier destination for adventure enthusiasts, featuring ski-in/ski-out access to Whistler Blackcomb. The hotel offers luxurious rooms and suites with breathtaking mountain views. The on-site adventure concierge can help plan a variety of activities, from skiing and snowboarding to hiking and mountain biking. Guests can also enjoy dining at Wildflower and Portobello Marketplace, as well as unwind in the Willow Stream Spa, fitness center, and outdoor hot tub after a day on the slopes.
