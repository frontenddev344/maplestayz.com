Explore Maple Stayz’s guide to top business hotels in Canada! For your work trips across the Great White North, our selection offers the ideal mix of efficiency and Canadian warmth. The Top Corporate Hotels In Canada

The Fairmont Royal York, Toronto

Price per Night: CAD $300+

The Fairmont Royal York offers historic charm and modern business amenities in downtown Toronto.

  • Prime location in Toronto’s financial district
  • Extensive meeting and event spaces
  • High-speed internet and executive services

The Fairmont Royal York is an iconic Toronto hotel offering elegant accommodations and comprehensive business facilities. Located centrally, it provides easy access to corporate offices and conference centers. The hotel features over 25,000 square feet of meeting space, including elegant ballrooms and high-tech conference rooms, making it ideal for both large events and intimate meetings. Guests can enjoy high-speed internet, a fully-equipped business center, and express check-in/check-out services. The on-site dining options, such as the Library Bar and Reign Restaurant & Bar, offer sophisticated environments for business lunches and dinners. Additional amenities include a fitness center, indoor pool, and a luxurious spa for relaxation after a busy day.
